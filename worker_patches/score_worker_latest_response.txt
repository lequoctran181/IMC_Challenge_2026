This is a **new narrow case5-only patch**. It does not run on `N≈23200`, `N>=200000`, or the broader lowband reopen/pre-W5 routes. It runs **after** the existing 81.69 fallback pipeline and rolls back unless the direct candidate passes `AF`, `W5::strong_validator()`, `vps(512)`, and has fewer vertices than the current fallback.

Hypothesis: the isolated `[47500,50625]` scoring case may be a cyclic tube/torus-knot mesh with `M == 2N` and row-major ring structure. The patch tries a **case5 tube-grid resample only under that exact feature gate**, with very high proxy thresholds. It is intentionally too narrow to diagnose/gain on that one case without risking high-N or lowband damage.

Expected score signal:

* **81.690173/7**: detector did not fire or proxy rejected; fallback preserved.
* **82.0–82.7-ish**: case5 fired and improved by roughly 2–7 case-points.
* **70.xx/7 or 6/7**: threshold still too loose; change `0.986` and `0.976` in `put()` to `0.992`, or disable by changing `band()` to `return 0;`.
